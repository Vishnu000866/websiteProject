About Nepal

Nepal is a country located in South Asia, bordered by China to the north and India to the south. It is famously known for its stunning Himalayan mountain range, including Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world.

"The highest reward for man's toil is not what he gets for it, but what he becomes by it." - John Ruskin

Key Attractions

  • Mount Everest - the world's tallest mountain
  • Kathmandu Valley - a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Lumbini - the birthplace of Lord Buddha
  • Pokhara - gateway to the Annapurna region

Interesting Facts

  1. Nepal has the only national flag that is not rectangular.
  2. It is home to 8 of the world's 14 highest peaks.
  3. Nepal was never colonized by any foreign power.
